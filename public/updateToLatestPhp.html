What is PHP and how does it affect my HelpDesk/ServiceDesk?

PHP is the programming language we use to build and maintain HelpDesk/ServiceDesk. Newer versions of PHP are both faster and more secure, so updating will have a positive effect on the performance of your site. Also from this version onwards we have deprecated our support for any version of PHP which is lower than 8.2. More Info

Note: Once you complete updating your PHP version to 8.2 and Ioncube loader to the recent version, please remove the updateToLatestPhp.html from your URL. After that, you will have to update your database.
